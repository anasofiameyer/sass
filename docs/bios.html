About the band

Harvey Dale: Frontman

"Harvey is the lead singer, guitarist and frontman of Harvey Dale and the Cements. He was born and raised in San Antonio, TX and learned to love music from his uncle who was a construction worker and brick layer. Harvey learned to love cement and music and eventually combined them when he started this band. Harvey attened the University of Texas and joined the organization XYZ."

Larry Potts: the Bassist

"Lawrence "Larry" Potts is the bassist for Harvey Dale and the Cements. Larry was born and raised in Frisco, TX but he didn't take an interest in music until his freshman year of college at the University of Texas at Austin. In his first semester, Larry joined XYZ where he met Harvey Dale. They quickly became best friends. Larry knew about Harvey's dreams and decided to help him by learning bass. Soon, they were having jam sessions and considering forming a band."

Finn Lynn: the Drummer

"Finn Lynn is the drummer of Harvey Dale and the Cements. He was born in Grand Rapids, MI before moving to Houston, TX. Finn always knew he was interested in music, learning how to play the guitar at a young age, but he was unsure on how he would pursue his love of music. Finn attended the University of Texas along with Harvey and Larry. Finn first met Larry and Harvey through XYZ, but they didn't become friends until later on, when he expressed an interest in their band."
